What is a Coding Boot Camp?

Technology moves fast, and advancements don't have time to wait for traditional four-year degrees. While there are plenty of theoretical computer science degrees still out there, the need for more developers has inspired a new generation of education and training - the Coding Boot Camp. Boot camps are short term, intense training sessions designed to prepare students for the practical reality of development. You can find full-stack boot camps, or those built for front-end or back-end development specifically. You can find UX/UI boot camps as well as data science or data analytics boot camps, and a host of other intense trainings. Whatever you want, there's a boot camp out there.
